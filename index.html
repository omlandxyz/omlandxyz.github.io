I am currently working as a research scientist in cryptography at the Norwegian National Security Authority.
Until 2019, I was an associate professor at Oslo Metropolitan University.
Before that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Oslo, and held a research fellowship through the Research Council of Norway.
Previously, I've been a visiting assistant professor at Arizona State University, and I got my PhD from NTNU.
For most of my academic career, my research interests have been operator algebras in connection with dynamical systems and group theory (see here for a recent research statement).
Here are some lecture notes for a course in C*-algebras.
More recent interests include cryptology and quantum information theory.
